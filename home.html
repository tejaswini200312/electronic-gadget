About the Project

This platform uses Machine Learning to assess electronic gadget addiction levels among students. By answering a simple 10-question survey, users instantly receive feedback on how their screen habits may be affecting them. Results are categorized into four clear stages—from healthy usage to high-risk addiction—helping students and educators better understand and manage digital behavior.

How It Works

🔐 Step 1: Register and log into the system using your credentials.

📝 Step 2: Take a quick 10-question survey focused on your gadget usage and digital habits.

📊 Step 3: Based on your responses, the system calculates a score and instantly classifies your addiction level into one of four stages.

📁 Step 4: Institutions can also upload bulk student survey data using a CSV file for group-level predictions and analysis.

Why Predict Gadget Addiction?

  • 🕒 Self-Awareness: Encourages students to reflect on how much time they spend on screens.
  • 💡 Healthy Habits: Promotes better digital hygiene and balanced screen routines.
  • 👩‍🏫 Early Intervention: Helps educators and counselors detect early signs of digital overuse.
  • 📢 Awareness: Spreads the importance of digital wellness in the academic environment.

Disadvantages of Gadget Overuse

  • 😴 Sleep Disruption: Late-night screen time affects sleep quality and posture.
  • 📉 Reduced Focus: Excess usage lowers attention span and academic performance.
  • 🙍 Social Withdrawal: Less real-world interaction can lead to isolation and mental fatigue.
  • 👁️ Health Issues: Causes eye strain, headaches, and increased anxiety levels.
